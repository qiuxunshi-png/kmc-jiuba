📋 Rules & Responsibilities · 经理权责说明
1
Submission Interval · 提交间隔Each report must be submitted within 24 hours of the previous one. Late submission requires explanation.每次提交须在上次提交后24小时内完成，逾期须向老板说明原因。
2
Variance Threshold · 差异阈值 ≤ 3%Cash and inventory variances must not exceed 3%. Exceeding this triggers an automatic alert.现金及库存差异不得超过3%，超出自动触发异常警报通知老板。
3
Purchase Receipt Required · 采购收据必须上传A receipt photo is mandatory for every purchase entry. No receipt = cannot submit.每笔采购必须上传收据照片，否则无法提交。
4
Data Integrity · 数据完整性Once submitted, data is locked. Modification requires owner's daily unlock code and a written reason. All edits are permanently recorded.数据提交后立即锁定，修改须输入老板当日动态码并填写书面原因，所有修改记录永久存档。
5
Manager's Commitment · 经理承诺Submitting confirms all figures are accurate. The manager accepts full accountability. Repeated discrepancies or unauthorized edits may result in disciplinary action.提交即确认数据真实准确，经理承担全部责任。反复异常或未授权修改将承担相应纪律处分。
